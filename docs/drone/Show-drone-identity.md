# Show drone identity

### [Show drone identity](Show-drone-identity.md)

_ref:_ [Drone radar design](Drone-radar-design.md) 

##### Function

Click on a drone mark on map and show the identity of the drone

##### Solution

When click on a drone mark on map, request from server drone information and show in a dialog beside the mark on map

**Show:**

1. DroneID
1. Speed and altitude
1. Drone type / name
1. Name of flyer / owner
1. Contact infor for flyer/owner (phone, mail, IM address etc)
1. Purpose of flight (amatour, recreational, press, etc etc) (
1. Camera yes/no
