# About Cantara

Cantara is Arabic for bridge, and we believe in creating value by bridging. Our goal is to strengthen and promote Norwegian software development by active collaboration with the government, the educational system, the Norwegian software marked and software communities. Cantara will facilitate a broad spectrum of knowledge-oriented services and projects. Cantara is a non-profit organization owned by Norwegian Software Development Foundation (Stiftelsen for fremme av programvareutvikling i Norge).

> Our mission:
>
> Mature and empower Norwegian software development.
