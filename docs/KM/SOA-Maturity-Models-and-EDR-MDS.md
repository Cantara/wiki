### SOA MM Level 1
- SOA is not WS-* and products but software design and architecture
- We have different categories of services, process and activity oriented services and resource-oriented services (A2A and ACS/CS)
- 

### SOA MM Level 2
- Data as key inhabbitants in your architecture
- We have orthogonal Business Data services and aggregated Business data services
- Design-time Governance
- The orthogonal data services master their business data on behalf of all coordinating parts

### SOA MM Level 3
- We have both activity and process services in A2A.
- SOA own the definition of our business data, and feed both the enterprise search and the DW with these definitions
