# Home

###### About Me

Currently I specialize in Maven, Continuous Integration and advanced testing and I put most of my thoughts and ideas in the [Agile2.0 space in Objectware Community Wiki](http://wiki.community.objectware.no/display/smidigtonull/Agile+2.0+Community+Home).

I have worked mostly with distributed systems and are familiar with challenges related to deployment, automated testing, automated deployment and configuration management.

I have a special interest in architecture and strive to learn as much I can about how to be a good architect. However, I do not call myself an architect (yet).
