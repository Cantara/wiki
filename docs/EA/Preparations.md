# Preparations

Preparations and recommended reading before the Geek Cruise

### Practical stuff

1. Pay the invoice for the cruise
2. [Check out the Qi4j codebase](http://www.qi4j.org/25.html) from svn and make sure it builds with maven (i.e. do a mvn install)
3. Read the [GeekCruise Agenda](/web/20221205134901/https://wiki.cantara.no/display/EA/GeekCruise+Agenda "GeekCruise Agenda") and the material mentioned there.
4. Read through the [background material](/web/20221205134901/https://wiki.cantara.no/display/EA/Qi4J%2C+EDR%2C+AOP+resources "Qi4J, EDR, AOP resources")
5. Bring your own laptop
