# Enterprise Architecture (EA)

From the [Wikipedia defintion](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enterprise_Architecture):  
Enterprise architecture is a term used to describe the practice of documenting the elements of business strategy, business case, business model and supporting technologies, policies and infrastructures that make up an enterprise.

The most well known enterprise architecture frameworks are [Zachmann ZIFA](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zachman_framework) and [TOGAF](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TOGAF).
