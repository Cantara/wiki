# P20. All services shall be in the service universe

### Motivation

Give the enterprise an understanding of the available services.

### Argumentation

We want to prevent duplication of services. A simple and consistent access to service documentation. Library documentation of already existing building blocks to ensure usage/reuse of existing investments.
