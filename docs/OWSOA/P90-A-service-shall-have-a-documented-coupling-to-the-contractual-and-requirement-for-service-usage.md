### Definitions

### Producer - Consumer requirement/contract

- Information requirements
  - data quality (freshness, correctness, validness/timespan...)
  - data usage rights (Licensing of data ++)
  - response time / availability
  - usability o.l.
  - peek (volume, traffic ..)
  - error handling/response

- Service Level
  - Expectations
  - Agreement
