"I know lots of architects who know architecture very well but are ineffective because of these other weaknesses. That's the person who talks in a monotone, and nobody listens to them because they're not very good communicators. You need someone with guts and passion. If they know architecture, this is very good too.

1. The Wrong Lead Architect
1. Insufficient Stakeholder Understanding and Support
1. Not Engaging the Business People
1. Doing Only Technical Domain-Level Architecture
1. Doing Current-State EA First
1. The EA Group Does Most of the Architecting
1. Not Measuring and Not Communicating the Impact
1. Architecting the "Boxes" Only
1. Not Establishing Effective EA Governance Early
1. Not Spending Enough Time on Communications
