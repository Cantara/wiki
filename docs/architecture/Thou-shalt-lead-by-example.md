# Thou shalt lead by example

The architect role, contains a significant/dominating component of **leadership as opposed to management**. This is closely related to creating a [learning organization](/web/20101024234143/http://wiki.cantara.no/display/architecture/Learning+organization "Learning organization"), [beeing an example](/web/20101024234143/http://wiki.cantara.no/display/architecture/Thou+shalt+lead+by+example "Thou shalt lead by example") and [communication](/web/20101024234143/http://wiki.cantara.no/pages/viewpage.action?pageId=2064587 "Thou shalt never forget that your day is 80% communication and 20% technology")

An architect must make an example through coding, direct communication and documentation, but is not giving orders on how to accomplish tasks at a detailed level. The architect should make an example on how to learn through experimenting and failing, and be a humble listener.
