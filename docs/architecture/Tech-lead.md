# Tech lead

###### Tech lead 

- Tech-lead is evaluated daily.  
    - Especially important to assign this role to _one_ person in an agile team. 

1. Responsibility and authority within a limited context. 
1. Can and should challenge rules and policies which the team feel are wrong. 
1. Developer can/should challenge the architect with controversial rules. Architect is responsible for screening these questions and take valid challenges back to the PAB.
